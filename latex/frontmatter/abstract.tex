\chapter*{Abstract}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Abstract}

The banking sector adopts OpenAPI contracts to expose business capabilities, while BIAN
standardizes reference models by Service Domain. However, there is no guarantee that a banking
OpenAPI contract faithfully reflects the reference business model. This research takes banking
OpenAPI contracts as the object of study and proposes an alignment model against BIAN Service
Domains through structural analysis, semantic similarity and concept coverage, expressed as a
reproducible AlignmentScore. The study is limited to documentary artifacts, without production
implementation or operational API security assessment.
